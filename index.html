RAINES Summer Seminar: Recent Advances in AI Problem SolvingK-RAINES Summer Seminar: Recent Advances in AI Problem Solving

Date: August 21, 2025
Location: Korean Consulate General in Boston
Speakers: Dr. Jungkoo Kang (IBM Research), Prof. Junehyuk Jung (Brown University & Google DeepMind)

On August 21st, we successfully concluded the K-RAINES Summer Seminar: Recent Advances in AI Problem Solving at the newly renovated Boston Consulate General! With a sold-out capacity of 55 attendees, this seminar was conducted with tremendous enthusiasm. Dr. Jungkoo Kang from IBM Research shared insights on LLM-based problem solving and workflow generation, while Prof. Junehyuk Jung from Brown University & Google DeepMind presented research on automated mathematical reasoning. We had meaningful time with insights from two speakers at the forefront of AI research, along with networking and dinner with participants. Thanks to the various prizes provided by the Boston Consulate General, we also enjoyed a fun raffle time, and most participants were able to take home a gift :) We thank the Consulate General of the Republic of Korea in Boston, Korea Evaluation Institute of Industrial Technology (KEIT), Rainbow Robotics, and all attendees who joined us for this seminar!
